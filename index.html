Work Experiences

Ohio Supercomputer Center

My term at Ohio Supercomputer Center started on June 2013 and ended on December 2013. Although I worked there for a short span of 7 months, I learnt loads of things. My manager, Thomas Bitterman was an excellent supervisor. Not only did he allocate interesting tasks to me, he also taught me a number of concepts behind the workings of the organization which I doubt anyone will do. Although I had done web development in the past, it is my period here that inspired me the most.

I worked on developing new websites and on enhancing existing ones. It was both a front-end as well as a back-end job and it was quite challenging. The webpages served as an interface for the end-users to run simulations on various models.

Cognizant Technology Solutions

I got placed in Cognizant by the time I graduated from Anna University. I started my job there in July 2011 and had an amazing run which ended in June 2012 because I wanted to pursue my master's degree. I worked as a Mainframe Developer for Cigna Healthcare's project. I was a part of the code cleanup team and also part of the data analysis team. My primary role was to identify dead code and to replace outdated code with current gen variants. I also wrote scripts to automate the process and introduced a quick solution to process instructions which had a similar pattern.
